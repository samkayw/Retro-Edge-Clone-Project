What We're All About

At Retro Edge Games, we're more than just a venue; we're your third place. But what exactly is a "third place"? In community building, the first place is your home, the second is your workplace or school, and the third place is where you go to unwind, socialize, and enjoy a sense of belonging.

That's exactly what we offer here at Retro Edge Games—a welcoming, inclusive space where gamers and geeks of Vallejo can gather, play, and feel at home.
